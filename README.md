Wealth Wellness Hub unifies traditional and digital assets into a single, secure dashboard — giving investors a complete, real-time view of their total financial health.

Today's investors face a fragmented reality: bank accounts, investment portfolios, CPF, and digital wallets all live on separate platforms, making it nearly impossible to see the full picture. Wealth Wellness Hub solves this by aggregating all asset classes into one interface, providing an instant snapshot of net worth, asset allocation, and spending behaviour.
Beyond aggregation, the platform embeds financial wellness analytics through a proprietary Wellness Score — evaluating liquidity, diversification, savings rate, and long-term readiness into a single, actionable metric. Users can track spending patterns, visualise portfolio risk, and monitor progress toward major financial milestones like retirement or property ownership.

Security is foundational, not an afterthought. A centralised security architecture wraps the entire application, ensuring consistent session monitoring and account protection across all features.
Built for Singapore's financial ecosystem and designed for young professionals navigating complex, multi-platform wealth, Wealth Wellness Hub transforms fragmented financial data into clarity — empowering users to understand their complete financial position, manage risk confidently, and build long-term financial resilience
